Who's on Station?

Anton Shkaplerov

Anton Nikolaevich Shkaplerov is a Russian cosmonaut. He is a veteran of four spaceflights and current Commander of the International Space Station.

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/

Pyotr Dubrov

Pyotr Valerievich Dubrov is a Russian engineer. He was selected by Roscosmos as a cosmonaut on 8 October 2012.

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/

Mark Vande Hei

Mark T. Vande Hei was selected by NASA in 2009. He is currently serving as Flight Engineer aboard the ISS for Expedition 65.

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/

Thomas Marshburn

Dr. Thomas H. Marshburn was selected by NASA in 2004. He is currently serving as pilot of the NASA SpaceX Crew-3 mission to the ISS.

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/

Raja Chari

Raja Chari was selected by NASA to join the 2017 Astronaut Candidate Class. He reported for duty in August 2017.

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/

Kayla Barron

Kayla Barron was selected by NASA to join the 2017 Astronaut Candidate Class. She reported for duty in August 2017.

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/

Matthias Maurer

In December 2020, Matthias was officially assigned to his first International Space Station mission known as "Cosmic Kiss".

Source: https://www.nasa.gov/
